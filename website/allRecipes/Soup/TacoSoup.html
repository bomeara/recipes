Taco Soup

Recipe from Heidi Jordan

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground Turkey
  • 1 Whole Onion
  • 1 Whole Package Hidden Valley Ranch
  • 1 Whole Package Taco Seasoning
  • 1 Can Black Beans
  • 1 Cup Sweet Corn
  • 15 Ounces Stewed Tomatoes (Mexican Flavored)
  • 15 Ounces Stewed Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Brown the meat and onions and drain.
  2. Mix ranch dressing mix and taco seasoning mix into the meat.
  3. Without draining, add all other ingredients.
  4. Simmer 1 hour.
Notes

Top with sour cream, cheese, corn chips as desired.
