Classic Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 8 Whole medium potatoes, cooked and diced
  • 1.5 Cup(s) mayonnaise
  • 2 Tablespoon(s) cider vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoon(s) sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon(s) yellow mustard
  • 1 Teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 Teaspoon(s) garlic powder
  • .5 Teaspoon(s) pepper
  • 2 Whole celery ribs, sliced
  • 1 Cup(s) onion, diced
  • 5 Whole hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 Pinch paprika

Directions

  1. Boil peeled potatoes in salted water until done. Cool to room temperature.
  2. Place diced potatoes in large bowl.
  3. Mix mayonnaise, cider vinegar, sugar, mustard, salt, garlic powder, and pepper in another bowl. Add to potatoes.
  4. Add celery and onions and mix well.
  5. Stir in eggs. Sprinkle a little paprika on top.
