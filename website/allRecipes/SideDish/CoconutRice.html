Coconut Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • .25 Cup(s) finely chopped onion
  • 1 Teaspoon(s) ginger
  • 1 Clove(s) garlic, minced
  • 1.25 Cup(s) jasmine or basmati rice
  • 1 Can(s) light coconut milk
  • .75 Cup(s) chicken broth
  • 1 Whole cinnamon stick
  • .5 Teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 Tablespoon(s) fresh lime juice
  • 2 Whole green onions, chopped

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add onion; cook 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  2. Add ginger and garlic, cook 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
  3. Add rice; cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Stir in coconut milk, broth, cinnamon stick, and salt; bring to a boil.
  4. Cover and simmer 18 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender. Remove from heat; let stand 5 minutes.
  5. Drizzle with lime juice and fluff with a fork. Sprinkle with green onions.
