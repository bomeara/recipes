Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 5 Dashes Angostura Aromatic Bitters
  • 3 Dashes Angostura Orange Bitters
  • 1/4 Ounce Simple Syrup
  • 2 Ounces Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 1 Whole Orange Slice

Directions

  1. Dash bitters into chilling glass.
  2. Squeeze orange slice into chilling glass, add simple syrup and whiskey.
  3. Place ice into chilling glass and stir. Allow to chill for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Strain into ball glass with ice. Garnish with orange or lemon twist and a maraschino cherry if desired.
Notes

Pure maple syrup can be substituted for simple syrup if you like to party or are Canadian.
