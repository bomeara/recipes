White Sangria

Recipe from Dan Wallien

Ingredients

  • 1 Bottle White Wine
  • 1/2 Cup Watermelon, Cubed
  • 1/2 Cup Honeydew, Cubed
  • 1/2 Cup Strawberries, Sliced
  • 1/2 Cup Fresh Raspberries
  • 1 Can Ginger Ale, To Taste

Directions

  1. Combine the white wine, watermelon, honeydew, strawberries, and raspberries in a large pitcher. Chill in refrigerator at least 1 hour to allow flavors to combine.
  2. Ladle wine and fruit into glasses, top with a splash of ginger ale to serve.
Notes

Meera recommends also adding white grapes and peach schnapps.
