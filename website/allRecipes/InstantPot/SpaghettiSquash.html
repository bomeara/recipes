Spaghetti Squash

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Spaghetti Squash
  • 1 Cup Water

Directions

  1. Cut spaghetti squash length-wise and put in pot on top of rack-stand or steaming basket. Add water to bottom of pot.
  2. Cook at high pressure for 8 minutes. Release pressure and remove squash meat from shell with fork.
