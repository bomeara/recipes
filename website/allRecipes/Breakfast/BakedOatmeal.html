Baked Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Oats
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/3 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Whole Eggs
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Vanilla
  • 3 Tablespoons Honey
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons Melted Butter
  • 2 Cups Frozen Mixed Berries

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. In a medium sized bowl, mix together oats, baking powder, cinnamon, salt, and brown sugar. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl mix together milk, eggs, vanilla, honey, and butter.
  4. Layer half the berries in a baking dish and top evenly with dry oat mixture.
  5. Pour in the wet mixture and add the rest of the berries on top.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until oats have absorbed the liquid. If using non-quick oats, increase baking time to 35-40 minutes.
