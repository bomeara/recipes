Avocado Chicken Salad

Recipe from Andrea Dietterle

Ingredients

Salad
  • 2 Whole Chicken Breasts, Cooked and Shredded
  • 2 Whole Avocados, Chopped
  • 1 Cup Corn
  • 2 Whole Green Onions, Chopped
  • 4 Whole Strips of Bacon, Cooked and Chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon Fresh Dill
Sauce
  • 1 Whole Lemon, Juiced
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Pinch Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Pepper

Directions

  1. Combine salad ingredients and sauce ingredients in two bowls and mix.
  2. Combine sauce and salad, mix lightly.
