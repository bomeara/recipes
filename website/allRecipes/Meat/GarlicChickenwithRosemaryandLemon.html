Garlic Chicken with Rosemary and Lemon

Ingredients

  • 2 Pound(s) chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 6 Clove(s) garlic, crushed
  • 3 Tablespoon(s) fresh rosemary
  • 3 Tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 Whole lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 Tablespoon(s) salt and pepper
  • .5 Cup(s) dry white wine

Directions

  1. Marinate chicken in all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Add chicken and marinade to baking dish, 9 by 13-inch.
  3. Roast 20 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes longer then remove chicken from the oven.
