Oven Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 3 Pounds Broiler-Fryer Chicken, Cut Up
  • 1/4 Cup Butter, Melted
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Dry Bread Crumbs
  • 2 Tablespoons Sesame Seeds
  • 1 Teaspoon Paprika
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Dried Thyme

Directions

  1. Dip chicken pieces in butter.
  2. Combine parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, sesame seeds, paprika, and thyme.
  3. Dip chicken into crumb mixture.
  4. Place on a greased 15x10x1 inch baking pan. Drizzle any remaining butter over chicken.
  5. Bake at 375 for 45-55 minutes or until juices run clear.
