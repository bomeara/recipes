Copenhagen Meat Balls

Ingredients

Sauce
  • 3 Tablespoons Drippings From Meat Balls
  • 4 Tablespoons Flour
  • 1 Whole Envelope Dehydrated Onion Soup
  • 8/3 Cups Water
  • 2/3 Cup Evaporated Milk
  • 1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • .25 Teaspoon Pepper
Meatballs
  • 1 Pound Lean Ground Beef
  • .5 Pound Ground Pork Sausage
  • .75 Cup Bread Crumbs
  • .25 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 1 Whole Egg, Beaten
  • .5 Cup Cold Water
  • .25 Teaspoon Nutmeg
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt

Directions

  1. Mix all meat ball ingredients well and shape into small meat balls, about 1.5 inches in diameter. Brown in butter or shortening in a heavy and large skillet about 8-10 minutes until done.
  2. Remove meat balls and prepare sauce in skillet.
  3. Return meat balls to sauce. Cover and simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Notes

Serves 6-8.
