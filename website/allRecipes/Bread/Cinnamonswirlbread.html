Cinnamon swirl bread

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup White Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Ground Cinnamon
  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Whole Egg, Beaten
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1/3 Cup Vegetable Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F.
  2. In small bowl, mix together 1/3 cup sugar and 2 teaspoon cinnamon; set aside.
  3. In large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, and remaining 1 cup sugar.
  4. Combine egg, milk, and oil, add to flour mixture and stir until just moistened.
  5. Pour half of the batter into a silicone bread pan.
  6. Sprinkle an even layer of approximately 2/3 of the cinnamon-sugar mixture over the batter.
  7. Pour remaining batter.
  8. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon-sugar on top.
  9. Draw knife through batter to marble.
  10. Bake in preheated oven for 45-50 minutes. Let cool for 10 or more minutes before removing from pan.
