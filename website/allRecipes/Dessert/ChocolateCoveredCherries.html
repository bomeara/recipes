Chocolate Covered Cherries

Recipe from Grandma Schuelke

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Powdered Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Margarine
  • 1/4 Cup Syrup
  • 1 Whole Jar Maraschino Cherries
  • 1 Whole Package Chocolate Chips
  • 2 Whole Squares Of Paraffin Wax

Directions

  1. Cream powdered sugar, margarine, and syrup together.
  2. Roll around cherries and chill.
  3. Dip in melted chocolate chips with some paraffin wax added(about a 2 inch square).
  4. The cherries with stems are easier to dip.
