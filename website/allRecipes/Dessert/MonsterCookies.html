Monster Cookies

Recipe from Colleen Wallien

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 1/2 Cups Peanut Butter
  • 3 Whole Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • 1 Teaspoon Karo Syrup
  • 2 Teaspoons Soda
  • 4 1/2 Cups Oatmeal
  • 3/4 Cup Chocolate Chips
  • 3/4 Cup M&Ms

Directions

  1. Cream first 4 ingredients. Add eggs, vanilla, and syrup. Add soda and oatmeal.
  2. Stir in chocolate chips and m&ms.
  3. Bake at 350 for 12 minutes.
