Bacon Jalapeno Deviled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 8 Whole Hard Boiled Eggs
  • 4 Tablespoons Mayo
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Sweet Relish
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Dijon Mustard
  • 5 Whole Slices Chopped Cooked Bacon
  • .5 Whole Jalapeno Finely Diced
  • 1 Pinch Worcestershire
  • 1 Pinch Cayenne
  • 1 Pinch Paprika
  • 1 Pinch Salt

Directions

  1. Once you scoop out the yolks from your boiled eggs, you place them into a bowl, and add the mayo, mustard, relish, and seasonings.
  2. Then you add in your cooked bacon and diced jalapeno, stir again, then spoon it into your eggs.
