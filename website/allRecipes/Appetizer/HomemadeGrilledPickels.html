Homemade Grilled Pickels

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Cucumber
  • 1 Cup Vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup Salt
  • 1/4 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons Dill

Directions

  1. Sliced a cucumber into decently thick pieces, a mandolin would have given me much to thin of slices I think.
  2. Grilled the cucumber slices over a very hot grill, I ended up placing them over a section that has a pretty big flair up to get a good char.
  3. Placed the grilled cucumber into a container and poured in just enough white vinegar to cover them. Then added roughly a 1/4 cup each of salt and sugar. And a few tablespoons worth of dill.
  4. After soaking in the vinegar for about half an hour I drained them and plated them. These suckers were amazing. Way better than what comes in a jar.
