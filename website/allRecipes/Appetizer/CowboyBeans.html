Cowboy Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Pork and Beans
  • 1 Can Lima Beans, Drained
  • 1 Can Red Beans
  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Ketchup
  • 1 Teaspoon Vinegar
  • 1/2 Pound Bacon
  • 1 Whole Small Onion
  • 3/2 Pounds Hamburger

Directions

  1. Put pork and beans, lima beans, and red beans in slow cooker.
  2. Stir in brown sugar, ketchup, and vinegar.
  3. Brown bacon, cut into small pieces and put into slow cooker.
  4. Brown hamburger and onion. When brown, add to slow cooker.
  5. Cook on high for 1 hour.
