Lobster Pasta

Ingredients

  • .25 Pound(s) salted butter
  • 2 Tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • .25 Cup(s) chopped garlic
  • 2 Cup(s) heavy cream
  • 8 Ounces(s) freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • .5 Pound(s) shrimp, cleaned and deveined
  • .5 Pound(s) lobster, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 Teaspoon(s) lemon juice
  • 1 Pinch salt and pepper
  • .25 Teaspoon(s) nutmeg
  • .25 Cup(s) chopped basil
  • 1 Pound(s) cooked pasta(of your choice)

Directions

  1. In large skillet slowly melt the butter over medium-high heat.
  2. While it's melting add the olive oil (this should keep it from burning). Butter/oil should be bubbly but not browned.
  3. Brown the garlic in the butter/olive oil, tossing to keep color even.
  4. Lower heat slightly and add the heavy cream& nutmeg.Stir, scraping bottom of the pan. Simmer for 3 minutes, stirring OFTEN.
  5. Once the cream has been incorporated thoroughly, add the cheese a little bit at a time. Before adding more cheese, make sure the last bit has melted into the cream mixture.
  6. Add the shrimp& lobster. Add salt& pepper to taste.
  7. Toss with cooked pasta& chopped basil.
