Pasta Salad Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 8 Ounces Rotini
  • .25 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • .25 Cup White Vinegar
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Garlic Powder
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Teaspoon Sugar
  • .5 Teaspoon Onion Powder
  • .25 Teaspoon Basil Leaves
  • .25 Teaspoon Dill Weed
  • .5 Cup Sliced Bell Pepper
  • .5 Cup Sliced Onion
  • .5 Cup Sliced Cucumber
  • .5 Cup Bite-Size Broccoli Florets

Directions

  1. Cook pasta as directed on package. Rinse under cold water; drain well.
  2. Mix oil, vinegar, and seasonings in small bowl with wire whisk until well blended.
  3. Toss pasta and vegetables in large bowl. Add dressing; toss gently. Cover.
  4. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Toss lightly before serving.
