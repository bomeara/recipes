Grilled Pineapple, chicken, and avocado salad

Ingredients

vinaigrette
  • .75 Cup vegetabe oil
  • .25 Cup cider vinegar
  • 3 Tablespoons honey
salad
  • 2 Whole chicken breasts
  • 2 Teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 Pinch salt and pepper
  • 1 Whole fresh pineapple, cut into 1- inch thick rings
  • 8 Cups baby spinach
  • 1 Cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 Whole avocado, diced
  • .5 Cup crumbled feta cheese
  • .25 Whole red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 Cups shredded and chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 Whole honey vinaigrette
vinaigrette
  • 2 Whole garlic cloves, minced

Directions

  1. Brush the chicken on both sides with olive oil, then season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat your outdoor grill to medium-high, or heat a grill pan over med-high heat. Place pineapple slices and chicken breasts on the grill. Cook for about 5 minutes per side or until the chicken is cooked through, and no longer pink on the inside.
  3. Remove pineapple and chicken and let cool for at least 10 minutes. Then slice chicken into strips, and cut pineapple into chunks.
  4. In a large bowl, toss together spinach, blueberries, feta, red onion, pineapple and chicken until combined. Drizzle or toss with salad dressing, and serve immediately.
  5. TO MAKE THE VINAIGRETTE: Whisk all ingredients together until blended. Let sit for at least 10 minutes for flavors to meld. Whisk again until blended, then drizzle over salad.
